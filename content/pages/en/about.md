OXOT is a Dutch cybersecurity company specialised in securing industrial environments where Operational Technology is critical to business continuity. We help energy, water, logistics and manufacturing organisations reduce operational risk and improve resilience — without disrupting production.

We understand that OT security is not about creating more reports. It is about helping organisations make better decisions. Everything we do is built to that end: OT-first thinking, risk-based prioritisation, and capability that stays inside your organisation after we leave.

## Why OXOT

Founded by former Fox-IT OT security leads, OXOT combines deep OT security expertise, hands-on experience in critical infrastructure, and advanced data-driven capabilities. The team brings experience from complex environments such as energy, water, logistics and manufacturing — places where a wrong move doesn't just lose data, it stops a process or endangers people.

That background shapes how we work. We reason about safety and continuity first, we respect operational constraints, and we treat cybersecurity as an engineering problem with a defensible answer — not a compliance checkbox.
