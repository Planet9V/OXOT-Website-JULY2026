## How we work

The same arc runs through every engagement — so a one-off assessment and a multi-site programme share one method and one source of truth.

```timeline
1 — Understand :: We map your assets, connectivity and operational constraints — the picture most OT estates cannot produce on request.
2 — Model :: We build a structured view of assets, risks and controls, increasingly held in a [Cyber Digital Twin](/en/cyber-digital-twin).
3 — Prioritise :: We rank gaps by real operational risk — not raw CVSS — so effort lands where it protects safety and production.
4 — Remediate :: We deliver improvement in proportionate, [IEC 62443](/en/iec-62443)-aligned waves that respect maintenance windows.
5 — Transfer :: We leave your team able to sustain it, with the model and the reasoning inside your organisation.
```
